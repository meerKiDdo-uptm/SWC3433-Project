DEAN

After his debut single [I'm Not Sorry] in 2015, DEAN is an alternative R&B artist that has collaborated with multiple artists such as Daniel Caesar, Anderson Paak., Syd, and Eric Bellinger, allowing him to grow as an internationally acclaimed artist. His influence reaches a wide range of scenes, from music to fashion to art in general. As the leader of alternative collective you.will.knovv, he relentlessly continues to create a new wave of trend.

Tabber

Tabber has proved his potential through collaborating with artists such as ROMderful, Punchnello, and Apro. He released his debut mix tape 'Deep End Mix Tape' in August 2020, making him one of the most exciting acts to watch.

Miso

Miso was the first Korean artist ever invited to participate in the renowned all expenses paid Red Bull Music Academy. She speaks her mind and shares her beliefs through her sincere lyrics and dreamlike melodies. Miso released her first single [Take Me] in December 2016. She released her second single "Alone" in May 2020. Miso's unique tone and genre bending musical style makes her an artist with fascinating energy and timeless taste.
